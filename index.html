About Us

Your Friendly Local Card Shop

Expressions is an independent card and gift shop on Station Road in Desborough. We stock a wonderful range of greeting cards for every occasion, gift wrapping, party supplies, balloons, and personalised items.

Loved by the local community, we take pride in offering a personal, friendly service with a selection you won't find in the big chains.
